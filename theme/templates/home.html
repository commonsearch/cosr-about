Set Common Search UI Demo as your Default Search Engine on Chrome (very early beta)

  1. Click on the Chrome menu icon on the top right of your browser
  2. Click Settings
  3. Under the Search header click Manage search engines
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the Other search engines section
  5. You should see three fields to enter a new search engine.

    Add a new search engine:
    Enter, "Common Search"

    Keyword:
    Enter, "commonsearch.org"

    URL with %s in place of query:
    Enter, "https://uidemo.commonsearch.org/?g=en&q=%s"

  6. Press enter on your keyboard
  7. Scroll up and find Common Search under the same Other search engines section you are already in
  8. Hover your mouse over the Common Search entry you just made, and click on the Make Default button.
  9. Congrats! You are now using Common Search as your default search engine :)
